Unified objective function (used by ALNS planner, applied to all methods):
  min F = C_tr * d_travel + C_ch * t_charge + C_delay * t_tardiness
        + C_conflict * t_conflict + C_standby * t_standby
        + C_unserved * N_unserved
  where N_unserved = N_total - N_completed (includes both rejected and unfinished)
  C_tr=1.0, C_ch=0.6, C_delay=2.0, C_conflict=0.05, C_standby=0.05, C_unserved=10000
